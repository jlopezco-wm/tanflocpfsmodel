### About
This calculator estimates the amount of Tanfloc SG required to clarify farm dairy effluent. Effluent pH is assumed to be be 6.5.

### Inputs
1. Effluent volume (litres): Litres of farm dairy effluent to treat. The value should be higher than zero. 
2. Effluent turbidity (NTU): This is the turbidity of the raw effluent in NTU. The value should be between 1000 and 30000 NTU. 
3. Tanfloc concentration (mg/L): This is the concentration of Tanfloc (on a dry matter basis) in the stock solution. The value should be higher than zero. If no value is input, the calculator assumes a value of 207 mg/L, which is the lowest observed in a stock solution with 25% Tanfloc (w:v). 
4. Objective: Choose between removing all turbidity(*maximum clarification*) or leaving a residual turbidity of 50 NTU (*moderate clarification*).

### Outputs
1. Dose: Estimated dose (mg/L) required to achieve the selected objective
2. Volume: Estimated volume (mL) of Tanfloc SG (25% w:v) required to achieve the selected objective
3. Acceptable range: 95% confidence interval. The average dose is very likely between the given range.

### About
This calculator estimates the amount of Tanfloc SG or Polyferric sulphate required to maximize turbidity removal from farm dairy effluent. Effluent pH is assumed to be be 6.5.

### Inputs
1. Coagulant: Select between Tanfloc and Polyferric sulphate
2. Effluent volume (litres): Litres of farm dairy effluent to treat. The value should be higher than zero. 
3. Effluent turbidity (NTU): This is the turbidity of the raw effluent in NTU. The value should be between 1000 and 30000 NTU. 
4. Coagulant concentration (mg/L): This is the concentration of Tanfloc or Polyferric sulphate (on a dry matter basis) in the stock solution. The value should be higher than zero. If no value is input, the calculator assumes a value of 207 g/L for Tanfloc and 475 g/L for PFS, which are the lowest observed in a stock solutions with 25% Tanfloc (w:v) or 50% Polyferric sulphate (w:v). 

### Outputs
1. Dose: Estimated dose (mg/L) required to achieve clarification
2. Volume: Estimated volume (mL) of Tanfloc SG or Polyferric sulphate required to achieve clarification
3. Acceptable range: 95% confidence interval. The average dose is very likely between the given range.
